### 题目：
$$
f_n = 
\begin{cases} 
1 & n = 1 \\
2n + f_{n-1} & n > 1 
\end{cases}
$$

### 步骤：
$$
\begin{aligned}
&f_n = f_{n-1} + 2n \\
&f_n = f_{n-2} + 2(n + (n-1)) \\
&f_n = f_{n-3} + 2(n + (n-1) + (n-2)) \\
&\vdots \\
&f_n = f_1 + 2(n + (n-1) + \cdots + 2) \\

&f_n = 1 + 2(n + (n-1) + \cdots + 2) \\
&f_n = 2 \left(\frac{(n-1)(n+2)}{2} - 1\right) + 1 \\
&f_n = n^2 + n - 1 
\end{aligned}
$$

### 渐近复杂度：
$$
O(n^2)
$$
